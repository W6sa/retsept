Tex Mex Hot Dog recipe ingredients

Ingredients Amount
Hot dog wieners 4pcs
Hot dog or sausage buns 4pcs
Corn salsa 1cup
Shredded cheese 1cup
Jalapeno peppers 2pcs

Directions

  1. Spoon equal amounts of corns salsa onto each bun
  2. Place once cooked wiener onto each bun
  3. Sprinkle with cheese
  4. Add grilled jalapenos
  5. Place prepared hot dogs into an casserole or baking pan
  6. Trasnfer to preheated 250F oven and bake for 10-15minutes until cheese has melted and bun is toasted
  7. Combine mayo, lime juice and cilantro and add to hot dogs as garnish
  8. Serve immediately
